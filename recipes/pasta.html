This Pasta Dish

Enjoy this delicious Tuscan chicken pasta recipe for lunch or dinner any day of the week.

Quick and easy creamy pasta recipe made Tuscan Style and ready in 30 minutes.

Easy and complete meal:making this Tuscan chicken pasta is so easy. Not to mention it’s all made in one pot and you don’t have to worry about making any sides.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Chicken
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Garlic Cloves
  • Almond Milk
  • Sundried Tomatoes
  • Baby Spinach
  • Flour

Steps For Making This Dish

  1. Boil and prepare pasta
  2. Prepare and cook the chicken
  3. Pour the milk mixture
  4. Whisk the milk and flour until no visible lumps
  5. Add spinach and cheese
  6. Enjoy your pasta!
